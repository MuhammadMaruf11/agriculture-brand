Applying Agrolead power prior to bloom at the right rate is critical for tree health and longevity, fruit yield and quality, and your profitability.

The most important parameter when selecting low-pH fertilizers is the bicarbonate (HCO3) in the irrigation solution. The use of low-pH
fertilizers is recommended if the water contains 100 mg/l or more of HCO3.
It is important to check the bicarbonate reduction capacity of each formula and to choose the one that best matches the specific water that a grower is using.
